Prediction of human housekeeping genes expression modifiers

Diseases and small molecules can affect the stability of housekeeping genes expression. To help users to identify putative housekeeping genes expression modifiers, HRT Atlas is integrated with three manually curated functional association data sets from Harmonizome database. These data sets provide relevant information about expression stability of human housekeeping genes based on differential expression, and are intended to guide users in the choice of the best candidate reference transcripts for qPCR normalization. A transcript must be avoided for normalization of qPCR if the disease or the molecule of interest appears in its gene modifiers list.
