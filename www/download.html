Download of housekeeping genes and reference transcripts

Users can download the complete list of human and mouse housekeeping genes and housekeeping transcripts. HRT Atlas also privides a list of the most stable housekeeping genes/transcripts across all non-disease tissues analyzed in the database and a list of housekeeping genes identified in both human and mouse databases. In addition, the full list of transcript-specific primer pairs of the proposed candidate reference genes has also been proposed.
